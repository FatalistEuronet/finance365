Our Mission
Equip students with financial knowledge and real-world skills so they can make smart decisions, build confidence, and be ready for life after high school
Our Vision
A future where financial literacy, leadership, and career readiness are part of every student’s toolkit—not just something learned by chance.
Our Values
- Accessibility: We believe financial education should be for everyone, not just those with access to special classes or resources.
- Collaboration: We learn better when we work together and share ideas.
- Growth: We challenge ourselves to step up, lead, and keep improving.
- Impact: We focus on practical skills that make a difference beyond the classroom.
- Creativity: We think outside the box when teaching and problem-solving.
What We Do
Financial Education 365 is a student-led, parent-guided nonprofit that helps middle and high school students build skills for the future. We host workshops, create videos, and run events to make finance, leadership, and career exploration engaging and approachable. Through our website and social media, students can learn: - How to manage money, save, and invest - Leadership skills through team projects and clubs - Career and college insights from guest speakers and mentors - Ways to volunteer and make a positive impact in the community Whether you’re a student looking to grow—or a parent ready to support—we welcome you to join our community.
