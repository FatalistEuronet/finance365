Get Involved

Want to get involved? You're in the right place! Volunteering with Financial Education 365 is a great way to learn new skills, meet other students, and make a difference in your community. You'll also earn volunteer hours for your participation.

How to Become a Volunteer

  1. 1. Sign Up
    Create an account by providing your name, school, grade, email, and other relevant information.
  2. 2. Confirmation
    We'll contact you by email with more information about getting started.
  3. 3. Get Involved
    After you register, you'll be able to sign up for events and activities through our website calendar. We'll also share other ways to volunteer once you've joined, so you can choose what interests you most.

Volunteer Hours

  • Hours from official FE365 events will be tracked automatically in our system.
  • If you do additional work outside of events (e.g., video editing, department projects), please track and record those hours on your own.
